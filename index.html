About Us

Manjara Charitable Trust’s Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology, Mumbai is a leading technological institute in Mumbai-Maharashtra (Western India). The institute is playing a key role in providing & promoting the engineering education and research programmes to students of Maharashtra & India. Established in 1992, the institute has a prestigious standing amongst the top engineering colleges in Mumbai, the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra. The young and dynamic leadership of Shri Amit Vilasraoji Deshmukh, The Secretary, Manjara Charitable Trust- Latur and the management of RGIT is committed to take forward the pioneering legacy of the visionary founder late Shri Vilasraoji Dagdojiraoji Deshmukh, who embarked on seemingly impossible goals towards the betterment of society and achieved them.

  • To create competent technical professionals with ethical behaviour and environment consciousness
  • Permanent affiliation to University of Mumbai.
  • Institution of Post graduate and research Programs in Computer, Electronic and Telecommunication and Mechanical Engineering.

The institute is located on the shoreline of Arabian Sea and at a picturesque location of the city. The campus exists at Survey No.161 & CTS No.1376/1/48, Juhu-Versova Link Road, Andheri (W), Mumbai-400 053. It is built on more than 5 acres of land alongside the green and beautiful Versova beach. Currently, the institute offers five UG, three PG and three research programmes in major disciplines of engineering. Being permanently affiliated to University of Mumbai and approved by All India Council for Technical Education (A.I.C.T.E.), New Delhi, the institute has been awarded “A” grade by the Govt. of Maharashtra. Currently, the institute offers following UG, PG and research programmes in five major disciplines of engineering. Three of its undergraduate programs are accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi, India for a period of three years starting from June 2014. A recent AICTE CII survey of 2017 has certified the institute as an Industry linked institute. Having achieved many awards and recognitions at state and national level, the institute is committed to be a Centre of excellence in academics and research in the coming years.

  • To facilitate at least 10 publication per department of original research papers by students and faculty every year in peer reviewed reputed journals
  • To enable graduating students to become entrepreneurs (10%), take up higher studies (40%) and on-campus placements (50%).
  • To motivate faculty to obtain 02 consultancy/ advisory assignments per year per department.

Computer Engineering

The department of Computer engineering was established in the year 1995. The prime focus of the department is imparting quality practical education. Our students are of high caliber and set to redefining the world of computing. The department gives equal importance to curricular and co-curricular activities for fostering the overall development of the students. The department conducts various seminars for preparing them for various aptitude tests and interviews.

Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at the MCT’s Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology provides quality education, conducts strong research programs, foster close partnerships with industry, and provides related services to the campus community and the community at large. A pillar of strength from which the department draws its strength is a team of highly qualified, experienced and dedicated staff.

  • 01 Vision

MANJARA CHARITABLE TRUST
FOUNDER HON. (Late) SHRI. VILASRAOJI DAGDOJIRAOJI DESHMUKH

(Former Minister of Science & Technology and Earth Sciences, Government of India and Former Chief Minister, Maharashtra, India)

In 1988, the great Indian visionary leader and philanthropist, late shree Vilasraoji Dagdojiraoji Deshmukh established Manjara charitable trust in Latur for providing quality education to students of Maharashtra. His inspiration and guidance enabled the educational and social institutions under the trust to achieve many recognitions and awards in the past and his social vision will be a motivation for all future initiatives and activities of the institutions under the trust.

The primary objective of the trust is to achieve all round rural and urban development through providing educational facilities in all major disciplines and areas. Moreover, through establishment of women educational institutions, the trust enables rural and urban women to get educated and actively contribute in development of nation. During last two decades, the trust started several educational institutions providing primary, secondary & Higher Secondary, Degree education and research programmes in almost all disciplines of knowledge at various places in Maharashtra. In 2008, Introduction of the Goldcrest chain of schools in Vashi –New Mumbai, Maharashtra, providing International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme (IDBP) and I.C.S.E. board system added another success story in the history of the trust. The trust has a legacy of establishing an environment where ‘excellence is habit and social welfare is mission’.
